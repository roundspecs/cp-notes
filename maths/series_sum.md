## Sum of natural numbers
$1+2+...+n=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}$
## Sum of square of natural numbers
$1^2+2^2+...+n^2=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$
## Sum of cube of natural numbers
$1^3+2^3+...+n^3=[\frac{n(n+1)}{2}]^2$
## Sum of arithmetic series
$\frac{a+...+b}{n}=\frac{a+b}{2}$
## Sum of geometric series
$S=a+ak+...+b$\
$\Rightarrow kS=ak+ak^2+...+bk$\
$\Rightarrow S-kS=a-bk$
